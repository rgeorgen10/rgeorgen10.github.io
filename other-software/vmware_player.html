VMWare Player

VMware Workstation Player is a virtualization platform based off of VMware workstation used for commercial use and costs money. Feature limitations in the player version include a limited number of snapshots and support for less guest operating systems.




System Requirements
  • Operating Systems: Windows 7 / 8 / 10
  • Memory: 2 GB
  • Storage: 1.5 GB
  • Processor: 1.3 GHz or faster Intel Pentium or later.
