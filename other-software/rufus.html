Rufus

Rufus is a portable application that extracts bootable ISO's to a USB flash drive instead of a DVD. Flash drives are not permanent unlike DVDs meaning that the flash drive can still be reused and formatted. Rufus is only available for Windows.




System Requirements
  • Operating System: Windows XP / Vista / 7 / 8 / 10
  • Memory: 512 MB
  • Storage: 1 MB
  • Processor: Intel Pentium 4 or later.
