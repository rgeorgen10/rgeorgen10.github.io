Bowling 101

Most people think that bowling is only about how good you can throw a bowling ball but there is much more to it. Bowling lanes have a specific amount of oil on them varying where on the lane there is more oil or less oil.
Areas with more oil will make a bowling ball go straight. Areas with less oil will allow the bowling ball to hook more. Good bowlers are able to choose a good line on the lane to play.
Every time a ball is thrown down the lane, a little oil is taken off the lane. This forces bowlers to have to adjust and change there stategy nearly every frame. This can also be countered by throwing different types of bowling balls. Some bowling balls will try to hook more, good for heavy amounts of oil. Other bowlings ball will try to hook less, good for less amounts of oil. Many bowlers will bring many bowlings balls into a bowling center to try to combat any lane condition.
