MakeMKV

MakeMKV is an open-source converter used for trans-coding video and even DVDs to the MKV file format. MakeMKV is still in the beta stage and doesn't work for every DVD. Blue-rays are also supported.




System Requirements
  • Operating System: Windows XP / Vista / 7 /8 / 10
  • Memory: 512 GB
  • Storage: 10 MB
