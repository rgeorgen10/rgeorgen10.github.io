Any Video Converter

Any Video Converter is a free web and file video converter supporting YouTube, and many other formats. Other features include a free CD-ripper, a video to audio converter. The following file formats are supported: MP3, AC3, OGG, AAC, WMA, M4A, WAV, APE, MKA, AU, M4B, AIFF, FLAC, DTS and more.
